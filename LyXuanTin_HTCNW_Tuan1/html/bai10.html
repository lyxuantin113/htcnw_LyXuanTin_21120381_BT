IUH

The forerunner of the Industrial University of Ho Chi Minh
University was Professional Training School in Go Vap and
founded December 1956. March 1999, the school was
upgraded to Ho Chi Minh University of Industry under
Decision No 214/2003/QD - TTg of the Prime Minister.
